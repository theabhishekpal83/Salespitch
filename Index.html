Consultative Sales Script
Ayush: Discovery Script
Add your script description here.
1
Opening
Make them comfortable · No pitch yet
"Hey Rohit, how are you doing? "
Ice breakers: "How is your day going so far?" , "I hope everyone is safe and sound around you?"
"This is Abhishek, I'm calling from Ayush's team regarding your interest in ML/AI remote jobs in the US, for that I want to have a discussion with you. 
To help you with your personalised career roadmap, I'd like to understand where you are, what your goals are, and see if there's something we can do that actually helps you."
Help me understand a little about you, what you do currently, your past experience and everything else :)
REMINDER
Don't pitch here. Your only job is to get them talking. Any answer they give is your next opening.
2
Dream/Aspiration/
Let them own the goal
"So this idea of getting a remote ML/AI job in the US, how long have you been thinking about it? Like when did it go from a random thought to something you actually started taking seriously?"
If they go quiet, try these
"Was there a specific moment? A conversation with a friend, something you read, maybe someone you knew who made that move and you thought - why not me?"
"You know honestly, most people I talk to have had this in the back of their mind for 6, sometimes 12 months before they do anything about it. Is that kind of how it is for you too?"
"Did someone around you get a US remote role recently? Because that usually triggers it for a lot of people."
3
Steps taken and what's stuck
Find the gap · Start building urgency
"Have you tried anything towards this so far? Applied anywhere, done some courses, talked to anyone about it?"
VARIATIONS
"I ask because some people come in knowing exactly where they got stuck, and others just feel like they don't even know where to start. Which one is more you?"
"Between skills, the actual job search, cracking international hiring, or just not having enough time — where does it feel the most blocked for you?"
"If I asked you right now, what's the one thing that's holding you back — what would you say?"
Use these when they answer
I hear that a lot actuallyThat Makes total sense
Yeah, That's a real gapRight, and that's honestly the hard part
That's exactly most people gets stuck on
4
What happens if this doesn't work out
Emotional anchor · This is your objection handling ammo
"Let me ask you something a bit more direct. If this doesn't happen — if a year passes and you're still in the same spot — what does that actually mean for you? Not just the career side, but where you thought you'd be by now."
Go deeper with these
"What's the cost of waiting one more year on this? Not money — I mean in terms of time, and where you want to be."
"You know I was just talking to someone recently who said exactly this — they kept saying next month, next quarter, and then two years had passed. Does that feel familiar?"
"If someone you started your career with is already working remotely for a US company right now — how does that sit with you?"
"You mentioned [their specific situation]. What happens to that if this doesn't move forward?"
Why this phase matters the most
Whatever they say here is your objection handling fuel later. If they say "I'll be stuck here with no growth" — when they later say "fee is too high" or "let me think," you come back: "You told me yourself what staying here costs you." Don't skip this phase. Don't rush it.
5
What does winning look like
Flip to aspiration · Let them visualise it
"Okay, flip side. Say this actually works out exactly the way you want. One year from now, you're working remotely for a US team. What does your life actually look like? Walk me through it."
Variations
"If you had to describe your ideal situation 18 months from today — role, pay, lifestyle — what would that be?"
"Forget the backup plan. What's the version of this that would make you feel like you actually nailed it?"
USE THESE WHEN THEY SHARE
That's a strong pictureI like that you're specific about this
That's completely achievableThat's exactly the direction we'd be building towards
That's the goal, let's work backwards from there
6
Ask for the CV/profile and set the meeting
Lock the next step

"This has been a really good chat. As a next step, I'm going to ping you on WhatsApp right now. Just share your resume there, and I'll send you the Google Meet link for "Mention the time here" 


We'll have a detailed discussion on your personalised career roadmap and next steps.

Have a good day !
